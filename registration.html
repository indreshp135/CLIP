Important Instructions

  • Registration fee includes registration kit, resource materials, food, conference proceedings (for authors) and participation certificate
  • Registration fees once paid will not be refunded back
  • Participants who wish to enroll should pay the registration fees through online portal and upload the registration form in the following Google Form link: Click Here
  • Papers once submitted and accepted for presentation (even with suggested revisions) cannot be retrieved back
  • Acceptance of submitted papers will be intimated through E-mail
  • At least one author should register as a presenter for Keynote cum Paper Presentation Session and present their paper. The remaining authors can register as an attendee (if they wish to attend the same). However, certificates will be issued only for those authors who have registered for the conference and attended the same without fail
  • Spot registrations are permitted on requisition basis (only for attendees)
