Call for Papers

Papers are invited for submission to the First International Conference on ComputationaL Intelligence Paradigms (CLIP) to be held at the National Institute of Technology-Tiruchirappalli, India from 3rd to 5th August, 2022.

The topics that are covered by the conference include, but are not limited to:

Language technologies

Special Issues

Extended versions of some selected papers may be considered for publication in special issues of different SCI indexed journals, after thorough reviews and following the norms and standards of the respective journals.

Proceedings

All accepted papers, whose author(s) register, would be included in the conference proceedings likely to be published by Springer International Publishing in its prestigious LNCS series.

Submission

Submissions must be made online through EasyChair following the submission link: Click Here. Papers should be of maximum eight A4 pages (including figures and tables) and complete with authors’ affiliations. Papers exceeding eight pages (limited to maximum 2 pages) will be subjected to over length page charges. Manuscripts must be prepared with LaTex2e (use Springer class file llncs2e.zip). Further details regarding submission, formats and downloads are available on the submission page.
