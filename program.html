Program

The conference comprises both Tutorial Sessions and Keynote cum Paper Presentation Sessions. Tutorial Sessions will be on day one and will be handled by both International- & National-level speakers. The Keynote cum Paper Presentation Sessions will be on the subsequent days. The keynote lectures will be delivered by experts from various esteemed institutions (like IITs, NITs, etc.), industries and faculties of NIT-T.
