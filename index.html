Conference Overview

The objective of this conference is to bring together the research personnel, like R & D / Industry personnel, faculty members, post doctoral fellows, research scholars and UG / PG students, from various organizations and to disseminate them the knowledge of state-of-the-art trends in artificial intelligence and machine learning. The event covers all the important aspects of a sensitive conference namely tutorial sessions, keynote sessions and paper presentations. As such, the conference will act as an energetic forum for a healthy discussion. The conference is being planned to be organized annually and this is the first conference of the series.
