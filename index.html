Conference Overview

The objective of this conference is to bring together the research personnel, like R & D / Industry personnel, faculty members, post doctoral fellows, research scholars and UG / PG students, from various organizations and to disseminate them the knowledge of state-of-the-art trends in artificial intelligence and machine learning. The event covers all the important aspects of a sensitive conference namely tutorial sessions, keynote sessions and paper presentations. As such, the conference will act as an energetic forum for a healthy discussion. The conference is being planned to be organized biennially and this is the first conference of the series.


About NIT-T

National Institute of Technology (formerly known as Regional Engineering College) Tiruchirappalli, is one among the premier Institutions of India and is well known for its high standards in teaching and research. It offers 10 undergraduate and 23 postgraduate Programmes in disciplines spanning Engineering, Science, Architecture and Management. It has been declared as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India under NIT Act. According to the Ministry of Human Resources Development, NIRF 2020, NIT Tiruchirappalli (NIT-T) has been ranked as the 1st NIT among all the NITs and 9th among all the engineering institutes in India. The Institute has signed MoUs with various Educational Institutions and Industries both in India and abroad as well with an aim to promote collaborative research and consultancy.
